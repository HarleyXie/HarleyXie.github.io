谢浩，中科院人工智能与大数据应用专家，中科院计算机硕士、北京大学工商管理硕士，法国巴黎文理研究大学PSL博士。
Xie Hao is an Artificial Intelligence and Big Data Application Expert at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, holding a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Chinese Academy of Sciences, a Master of Business Administration from Peking University, and a PhD from Paris Sciences & Lettres – PSL University.

拥有20年IT行业技术研发与管理经验，历任央企产品研发副主任、大数据及人工智能初创企业副总裁/CEO、中科院研究室副主任等核心职务。
With 20 years of experience in IT industry technology research & development and management, he has held key positions including Deputy Director of Product R&D at a state-owned enterprise, Vice President/CEO of big data and artificial intelligence startups, and Deputy Director of Research Laboratory at the Chinese Academy of Sciences.

多次担任部委科技项目技术评审专家、行业专家委员会委员，参与或主持多个国家重大信息化项目。
He has served multiple times as a technology evaluation expert for science and technology projects under various ministries and commissions, and as a member of industry expert committees. He has participated in or led multiple major national informatization projects.

主导团队取得近百项核心自主知识产权，助力多家机构实现数字化转型和业务智能化，完成亿元以上科技成果转化，在人工智能产业化应用领域具有丰富的理论基础和实践经验。
Leading teams to obtain nearly 100 core independent intellectual property rights, he has helped multiple institutions achieve digital transformation and business intelligence, completed technology transfer worth over 100 million yuan, and possesses rich theoretical foundation and practical experience in the field of artificial intelligence industrialization and application.
